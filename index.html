    • About

    Cheylea is a Principal Data Analyst for Adult Social Care at Southampton City Council, running a team of analysts to deliver reporting requirements and statutory returns.

    Her specialty is shaping and growing company-wide reporting to drive business decision using an evidence based data approach and working with stakeholders to understand what type of evidence can aid this.

    She is passionate about always learning, from self teaching many of her technical skills to now currently studying a Masters degree in Computer Science at the University of Liverpool to formalise her software development and delve deeper into the world of Artificial Intelligence.

    For her undergraduate degree, she was most interested in number theory, algorithms and statistics and uses these concepts to feed into her day to day career.

    She is an avid musician, self described as "playing a lot of instruments badly" and running choirs in both university and prior to 2020 in her local community.
