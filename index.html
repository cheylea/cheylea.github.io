    About

    Hi! I'm the Principal Data Architect for Southampton City Couincil (SCC), running a team to deliver robust data marts to all services across the city and supporting research work as part of the Health Determinants Research Collaborations. I have previously led the data team for Adult Social Care at SCC and I've worked on various data-related projects throughout my time at the council and across my career.

    My speciality is shaping and growing company-wide reporting to drive business decisions using an evidence-based data approach. To execute this, I work with stakeholders to understand what type of evidence can aid and empower people with data.

    Having had the opportunity to work on interesting projects throughout my career, I've worked on various flavours of data projects. This includes building datamarts, working on classification machine learning algorithms and partnering with colleagues to deliver a single source of truth for council-wide key performance indicators.

    Continual learning and self-improvement is my passion, from self-teaching SQL in a week when I decided I wanted to be an analyst to currently studying for a master's degree in Computer Science at the University of Liverpool. This has allowed me to delve into code deeper than I ever thought I could - and I recently completed my dissertation project looking at a blockchain method for electronic voting.

    Mathematics was my chosen subject at undergrad, and I favoured number theory, algorithm design and statistics - using many of these concepts daily.

    In short, I love getting stuck in to a new challenge and am content as long as I get to use my skills to solve cool problems.

    Work Experience at Tando, 2021

    While working for Tando, I started as a Data Analyst and Project Manager and soon took on the extra role of Head of Teach and lead for the 5-person tech team.

    My role as Data Analyst was primarily building and managing business-wide reporting for stakeholders to create visibility across channels (SQL, Power Query and Power BI).

    My Project Manager role involved writing up technical specifications for projects, translating to business requirements required by the business, and working directly with developers to execute them, often with swift turnarounds, working across several time zones.

    The Head of Tech role included running the daily stand-ups, ticket organisation and delegation for developers, and working Agilely using Azure DevOps.

    Work Experience at ClearLoans, 2020

    ClearLoans was my first Data Analyst role and involved building the reporting for the whole business in Power BI, alongside using SQL in Microsoft Azure Data Factory. Reporting consisted of various areas such as payout and collections information.

    Whilst at ClearLoans, took on many analysis projects such as creating an income analysis algorithm for accurately capturing an applicant's income, a bank statement transaction classification model using machine learning and a credit risk-weighted scorecard to identify customers likely to default on their loans.

    All projects involved close collaboration with developers to implement models into production and thinking outside the box with limited resources.

    Work Experience at Onefeed, 2018

    My first role after graduation was as an Account Manager for over ten clients, devising marketing strategies for the Pay-Per-Click Google Shopping and Bing Shopping campaigns and monitoring product feeds for errors and data quality. The role involved regular performance analysis, including using Google Analytics and working directly with clients to understand their products.

    Whilst working at Onefeed completed various exams, including Adwords Fundamentals, Shopping and Search exams and Bing Shopping. Here, I developed my Excel skills and began to foster an interest in SQL when working with the developers. I also took on the additional role of admin assistant, producing employee documents and training guides to assist with onboarding.
